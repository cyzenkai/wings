Safety and Hazard

Expert receives the call and identifies it as a Safety and Hazard incident if it falls under any of the scenarios:
  • Customer reporting injury or property damage
  • Arlo product has smoke/fire
  • Water damage/corrosion/melted/deformed
  • Battery leaking/battery swollen
  • Arlo product too hot to touch

L1: Create a case, escalate by warm transferring to CCT.
