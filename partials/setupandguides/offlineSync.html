Offline Syncing

  1. Turn off the router or bring the SmartHub to a different room away from the router.
  2. Plugin the SmartHub to power source and wait for the LED to start blinking amber.
  3. Remove the casing of the camera.
  4. Press the sync button of the SmartHub for not more than 2 seconds. SmartHub LED will stay blinking amber.
  5. Press the sync button of the camera for not more than 2 seconds. Camera LED will blink blue slowly then turns to rapid blinking blue.
  6. Turn on the router and plugin the SmartHub to the router.
  7. Wait for the SmartHub to be online, log into the App and complete the camera on boarding /check CS diag tool if the camera is synced now.
