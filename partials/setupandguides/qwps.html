Arlo Q Installation with WPS

  1. Initiate installation as normal. When you get to the Verify WiFi password screen, enter any SSID and password.
  2. Press and hold the sync button on the Arlo Q until it blinks blue. Click/tap continue.
  3. On this page, DO NOT SCAN THE QR CODE. Click/tap continue.
  4. Press and hold the WPS button on the router until it blinks. The Arlo Q should chime once it links with the router.
