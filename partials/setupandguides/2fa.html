2-Factor Authentification

Push Notifications
  1. Open Arlo App and login, Tap on Ellipsis button
  2. Click/tap Settings
  3. Click/tap Profile
  4. Click/tap Login Settings
  5. Click/tap Two-step Verification
  6. Toggle Enable
  7. Check Push Notifications, Click/Tap Continue
  8. Click/Tap Trust
  9. Choose a name for the device, Click/Tap Continue
  10. 2 Factor Authentication is enabled for the device.
