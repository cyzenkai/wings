Android App Installation

Playstore
  1. Tap on Play Store.
  2. Play Store screen.
  3. Search for Arlo. You can click on the arlo app if it shows. If not, tap on the magnifying glass.
  4. Tap on Arlo with Arlo Technologies, Inc.
  5. Tap on Install.
  6. Arlo App will download.
  7. Arlo App will install.
  8. Once finished, tap on Open.
