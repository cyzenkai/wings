Arlo & Verisure Announcement

At 1PM PT today, Arlo issued a press release on International Newswire announcing that Arlo and Verisure have entered into agreements to include the acquisition of Arlo’s European commercial operations and a strategic supply partnership for Arlo camera’s.

The strategic partnership will create the first European multi-channel go-to-market strategy for consumer security and surveillance services by adding Arlo’s retail and e-commerce channels to Verisure’s existing direct sales channels.

For any inquiries about this news, please direct our customers to our Press page.

Problem: Homekit Installation
Symptoms: Unable to discover camera using Homekit app
Root cause: Others (Be very specific in case notes)

L1
Please check with the customer the following:
  1. When did the customer notice the issue?
  2. Get screenshot of the error that the customer is getting.

Expert to do:
  1. Inform the customer that we are aware of the issue and we are working on a FW fix.
  2. Escalate case to L2 for issue attachment.

    3. *If the customer cannot wait for FW fix, escalate the case to L2 for Homekit token reset request.

    L2
    1. Validate that it is really related to ISSUE-10719.
    2. If customer cannot wait and Homekit token reset is requested, please escalate case to L3 with the following info:
      • Case number
      • Account email address
      • Affected BS SN

    L3
    1. Request for Homekit token to be reset for the customer to DL-CloudOps@arlo.com
    2. Once confirmed that Homekit is reset, de-escalate case to L2.
