Video Doorbell Possible Low Battery at Onboarding

 

Scenario

Customer states that the Arlo Video Doorbell is not exhibiting a solid white LED ring indicating it is ready for setup and has confirmed that the breaker is turned on.

Symptom/Issue

Customer has completed connecting the Power Kit to the chime, connected the existing wiring to the back of the Video Doorbell and confirmed that the breaker has been turned on and does not see the LED ring turn solid white.

The LED ring on the front of the Video Doorbell provides information on the doorbell state:

SOLID AMBER – Voltage is too low, customer should upgrade their transformer

FLASHING AMBER / WHITE – Internal battery is low, customer should leave doorbell connected to AC power for 15 – 30 minutes and check that that the LED ring transitions to solid WHITE

NO LED – Power is not connected, customer should verify the wiring did not come loose or disconnected when mounting the doorbell.

See: https://kb.arlo.com/000062326/What-do-the-LED-behaviors-on-my-Arlo-Video-Doorbell-mean

 

Confirmation of Issue

Customer performed a second check on all connections and ensure the breaker is turned on but still does not see the LED ring turn solid white.

Action

At this point, it may possible that the customer has a unit that may be affected by a less than sufficient charge to begin the onboarding process and needs to be charged.

Gather the serial number from the customer’s Video Doorbell.

Check if the Video Doorbell serial number is present in the affected Video Doorbells list (Refer to: ‘Video Doorbell – Possible Low Battery SN‘ Smartsheet: https://app.smartsheet.com/sheets/fPF488FMxQxHcwmPPP6M2664vWxQpvR5cWph2MH1?view=grid

 

Solution

If the serial number is in the list of affected devices, inform the customer that the Video Doorbell’s internal battery most likely needs time to be charged in order to begin the onboarding process. It may take between 15 to 30 minutes to charge to a sufficient operating level.

Offer a call back to see if the customer needs additional help after the Video Doorbell has had the appropriate amount of time to charge.
