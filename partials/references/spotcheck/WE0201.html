=========================================================================================================================== FYI: What is California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)?

 

On January 1, 2020 California has implemented a new law similar to GDPR where California residents have the right to:

 

Know what personal data is being collected about them.

Know whether their personal data is sold or disclosed and to whom.

Say no to the sale of their personal data.

Access their personal data.

Request the business to delete any personal information about the consumer which the business has collected from the consumer.

Not to be discriminated by exercising any of the consumer‚Äôs rights.

 

Arlo Team will post a statement on our site on how customers can submit their questions and/or request to exercise this right.

 

A different dedicated phone number and/or email address will be posted but just in case you received an inquiry pertaining to this, kindly follow the GDPR process.

 

Currently, we are in the process of adding CCPA as a disposition code for documentation and we will share once it's done.
