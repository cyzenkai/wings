Specification Details

Arlo’s first video doorbell, packing high performance into a cost-effective platform.

The Arlo video doorbell offers a superior user experience, with much wider field of view, optimized for the doorbell – allowing customers to clearly see from package to person and across their entire front entry.

Specifications:
  • 2MP HD 1536x1536 resolution with HDR
  • Ultra-wide 180° field of view
  • Works without an Arlo base-station
  • Direct-to-Mobile SIP Video Call
  • Advanced Noise Reduction
  • 2-way conversational audio
  • Built-in Tamper Detection and Siren Capability
  • Motion Detection and Instant Alerts
  • Easy DIY Wired Power, 16VAC – 24VAC
  • Night Vision
  • Weather-Resistant IP65-rated
Product Dimensions:

  • in: 1.77 x 5.11 x 0.98
