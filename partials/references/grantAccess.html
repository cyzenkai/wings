Edit Privileges


  1. Launch the Arlo app or log in to your Arlo account at my.arlo.com.
  2. Tap or click Settings.
  3. Under account, tap or click Grant Access.
    4. A list of your friends displays with the status of their accounts.
  4. Tap or click a friend and Edit.
  5. Tap or click cameras to select or deselect them.
  6. Move the Allow Access Rights slider to the right to grant your friend more privileges.
    7. Note: When you grant access rights to your friends, they can change your account and camera settings.
  7. Tap or click Done.
    8. Your settings are saved.

Access Rights Privileges


Friends who are granted access rights can do the following:
  • Play and pause video footage
  • Record video footage
  • Mute the speaker
  • Enter full-screen view
  • View live footage
  • Zoom and drag video footage
  • Control brightness
  • Access and change modes
  • View motion detection statuses
  • Manually record
  • Take snapshots
  • Favorite, share, download, and delete video footage
  • Use a camera's microphone

Friends without access rights can do the following:
  • Play and view library content
  • View live footage
  • Zoom and drag video footage
  • Control brightness
  • Enter full-screen view
