Night Vision

  1. Remove Camera From Account.
    • Login to your account.
    • Go to settings. Click my devices. Select the camera.
    • Hit remove device button.
  2. Power cycle Base Station.
    • Unplug the base station from power source. Wait for 1 minute.
    • Plug back in base station to power after 1 minute.
    • Wait for the 3 green LED's on the base station.
  3. Sync the camera.
    • Press the sync button on the base station, just once then release. The 3rd LED from the left will flash green.
    • Press sync button on the camera, just once then release. A blue LED will blink blue slowly then rapidly.
  4. Place the camera inside a dark room or a closet then stream the camera live. Try adjusting its brightness as well. It's night vision should work now.
  5. Turn Night vision on and off.
    • Click or tap Settings. The Cameras page displays.
    • Click or tap My Devices.
    • Click or tap the Garage camera.
    • If you are using the Arlo app on a mobile device, tap Video Settings.
    • Click or tap Night Vision.
  6. If it shows the same thing, take screenshot then try to place the affected camera together with a working one (provided that he has more than 1 camera) inside a dark room or a closet then stream the camera then check the difference. If it shows significant difference, take screenshot and attach it on the case.
