Pink Tint

Customers might see an occasional image color distortion on the Arlo Wire-Free camera.

Symptom:
  • The images are tinted red, but the image might also be tinted with other colors.
L1 Expert:
If the customer experiences color distortion in images from an Arlo camera, make sure to:
  1. Get the Camera SN showing the defect from customer or CS diag tool
  2. Log a ticket against the ‘faulty’ camera
  3. Use the following codes to log the ticket:
    1. Problem: Performance
    2. Symptoms: Camera – Image appears distorted color (red)
    3. Resolution: (this must be updated at the end of the case based on the case outcome)
      1. Power cycled the camera
      2. RMA camera
      3. Advised firmware upgrade
  4. Verify the following:
    1. Camera is on latest firmware.
    2. Reset the camera by opening and closing battery door.
    3. Verify camera is working following the correct specs (ie. Right temperature/humidity, swap camera around and check if the issue follows the camera)
  5. Hardware warranty: Do not follow hardware warranty for red tint cases/issues. If the customer can prove that their camera is having red tint issues and is outside of hardware warranty, then log an RMA for the customer.
  6. If the problem persists, inform the customer that we will ship out a replacement camera.
    1. Provide instructions for the customer to upload an attachment to the case; showing evidence of red image problem. A snapshot or a short video will satisfy this requirement. GDPR compliance: Instruct the customer to ONLY upload a picture or video without any human beings in it. Example: Picture or video of an empty room, an empty backyard, or picture/video of an empty walkway.
    2. Take all the customer’s details to setup the RMA.
    3. Escalate to TL/TM.
TL/TM:
  1. Verify case logging and the troubleshooting was done correctly.
  2. Issue can be seen in both cut filters old and new.

    3. 48B prefix (old cut filter)
    4N7, 4WA, or 5EX prefix (new cut filter)

  3. For NA (Tracelogix) - Log RMA by using 'Advanced Ground - Home, Adapters&Antennas (Shipping Waived)'
  4. For EU (ANOVO) Log RMA by using 'EU - Advanced Ground - All (2-3 day, Call for collection - shipping waived)' option and Exception 99 to specify both SN Prefix 4N7 and 4WA (Anovo will ship whichever available).
  5. For UK (ANOVO) Log RMA by using 'UK - Advanced Ground - All (2-3 day, Call for collection - shipping waived) option and Exception 99 to specify both SN Prefix 4N7 and 4WA (Anovo will ship whichever available).
  6. For APAC (Brightstar) Log RMA by using 'AU - Advanced Express RA (Shipping Waived) option.
