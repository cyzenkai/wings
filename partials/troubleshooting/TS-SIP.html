For iOS:
  1. Navigate to phone "Settings"
  2. Scroll down to "Privacy"
  3. Select "Microphone" and "Camera"
  4. Ensure that the Arlo app has permissions enabled
For Android:
  1. Navigate to phone "Settings"
  2. Select "Apps"
  3. Scrolld down to "Arlo"
  4. Ensure that the Arlo app has "Microphone" and "Phone" permissions granted
