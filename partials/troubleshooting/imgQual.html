Image Quality Issues


  1. Change the Power Management setting to Best Video
    • Launch the Arlo app or log in to your Arlo account at arlo.netgear.com.
    • Tap or click Settings > My Devices.
    • Select the camera whose power management settings you want to change.
    • Tap or click Video Settings.
    • If you are using the Arlo app on your mobile device, scroll down to POWER MANAGEMENT and select a power setting: For the best video quality, select Best Video. This setting reduces battery life but provides the best video quality.
  2. Check your speeds
    • Access http://www.speedtest.net/ by Ookla
    • Click on GO or begin test
    • Please indicate your upload and download speeds

    3. If this doesn't improve the video quality:
  3. Remove Camera From Account.
    • Login to your account.
    • Go to settings. Click my devices. Select the camera.
    • Hit remove device button.
  4. Sync the camera.
    • Press the sync button on the base station, just once then release. The 3rd LED from the left will flash green.
    • Press sync button on the camera, just once then release. A blue LED will blink blue slowly then rapidly.
