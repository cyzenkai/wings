Note to Customer: Please return the charger, battery(ies) and power adapter
NOTE: SHIPPING DAMAGED BATTERIES IS STRICTLY PROHIBITED
Under these conditions, shipping regulation will not allow return of batteries:
  • The battery or enclosure is cracked or broken
  • The battery or battery enclosure shows indication of melting or deformation
  • The battery is leaking any type of fluid
  • There is a strange odor coming from the battery (typical odor of concern would be a sweet, solvent-like smell)
  • The battery is hot, too hot to be comfortably handled by the customer (if the battery is just warm and can be easily handled by the customer, this is acceptable for shipping)
  • The battery has been burned or exposed to fire
  • The battery enclosure shows signs of heat damage (darkened coloration, burn marks, etc.)
  • The battery is swollen
